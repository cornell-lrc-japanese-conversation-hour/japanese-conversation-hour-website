Sara Goto

イメチェン
[ime̞tɕe̞ɰ̃]

(n) derived from “image change”, refers to the changing of one's appearance to give off a distinct and new impression. Think JoJo Siwa.

メロい
[me̞ɾo̞i]

(adj) usually directed towards an idolized person, meaning “so cool/cute/charming (as to make myself enamored)”, formed from the adjectival noun (形容動詞) “メロメロ“ [me̞ro̞me̞ro̞] (enamored, smitten, head over heels).

解釈違（かいしゃくちが）い
[käiɕäkɯ̟t͡ɕigäi]

(n) also usually directed towards an idolized person, literally meaning “difference in interpretation”, used in contexts where the idolized version of a person appears to be different from their actual self.

反転（はんてん）アンチ
[häɰ̃te̞ɰ̃äɰ̃t͡ɕi]

(n) refers to an “anti” (someone who obsessively hates an idolized person, ~hater) that used to be a fan, “反転” meaning reverse. Also called “ファンチ” [ɸäɰ̃t͡ɕi] (from fan + anti).

EXAMPLE SCENARIO

Aちゃん　「ずっと推してきたC君、最近すっごいイメチェンしてさ。さわやか清楚系（せいそけい）から離れようとしてるのかな？？」

Bちゃん　「そだね、でもそれはそれでメロいじゃん。ストーリーの自撮りとかメロさ大爆発（だいばくはつ）だったじゃん」

Aちゃん　「うーん、でも古参（こさん）としてはちょっと複雑（ふくざつ）かな。最近の恰好見てるとすんごい解釈違いに感じるの。私が厄介（やっかい）なだけかな？でもきっと他のファンの子も思ってるよね？」

Bちゃん　「典型的（てんけいてき）な反転アンチになりそうで怖いわ」



TRANSLATION

A　“My fav C I’ve been following for so long recently changed their whole style. Are they tryna detach themselves from their innocent image??”

B　“Yeah, but they’re still serving cunt. Like their Insta story selfie was so slay”

A　“I guess, but as an old stan it’s still a lil weird. Seeing how they dress recently, it’s just not them at all. Am I just one of those annoying fans? But others def feel like this too right?”

B　“Oh my god you’re literally becoming an anti”
