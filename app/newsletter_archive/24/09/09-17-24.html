2024's Rising J-Pop Artists
Tami Takada

At the beginning of 2024, Spotify revealed a list of 10 new artists to keep an eye out for this year. Here, we introduce 3 of them!

jo0ji

jo0ji, a 24-year-old fisherman from Tottori prefecture, debuted in 2023 with「不屈に花」. Although he has been practicing piano from second grade, jo0ji didn’t start making his own music until urged to by a friend. From there, jo0ji began writing alternative pop and has been praised for his unique voice.
「escaper」 「眼差し」

FLiNR

First Love is Never Returned is a five person band from Hokkaido prefecture headed by vocalist Kazuki Ishida. Ishida started performing in his college days and quit his job to study Black music—everything from 30s jazz to 90s hip hop—in New York. After returning to Japan, Ishida started this urban pop/R&B band with bassist Yuji Sato and began recruiting other members.
「Unlucky!!」 「夜的平成浪漫」

十明 (Toaka)

Pop singer Toaka, currently a college senior, built her following by releasing song covers on TikTok. She debuted in 2023 with 「灰かぶり」after singing the theme song for Shinkai Makoto’s movie すずめの戸締り [Suzume] (2022).
「NEW ERA」 「Dancing on the Mirror」
