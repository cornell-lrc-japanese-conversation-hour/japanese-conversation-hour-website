What is Conversation Hour?

Japanese Conversation Hour (和会話教室), hosted by these facilitators, offers an hour of casual conversation in Japanese at your most comfortable level. We are open to anybody—you can drop in and just chill too—but we encourage active listening and speaking to maximize the benefits of learning. We're excited to meet you!
